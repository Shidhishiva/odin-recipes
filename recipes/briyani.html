Briyani-Recipe

Description

This chicken biryani recipe has been one of the most requested reader recipes and I'm so excited to share it with you today. Succulent, juicy pieces of chicken are cooked in a yogurt marinade and then layered with crispy onions, coriander, mint and basmati rice to give you a dish that the entire family will enjoy.

Easy Tips to Make Perfect Biryani

Before starting, I wanted to share 10 tips to make tasty biryani that you eat in restaurants. Some basic hacks will help you to create perfect taste:

  1. Thinly slice the onion. Thick onion slices will not make tasty curry.
  2. Use fresh ginger garlic paste.
  3. Be accurate in using spices
  4. Fully cook your meat.
  5. Use Basmati rice to make presentable and aromatic.
  6. Be careful, when you soak biryani rice. (Don't soak more than 15 minutes if you are using basmati rice)
  7. Fully drain the water from boil rice before making the biryani layer.
  8. Rice should be boiled 90% before making the layer. (Never fully boil biryani rice before making layer)
  9. When making the layer, make sure the curry should not be watery. If it is then cooked on high heat.
  10. Make sure when biryani get ready, then don't run the spoon from the middle. Alway run spoon from sides to avoid rice break.

Ingridents of Briyani

A proper quantity of ingredients is always important to make perfect biryani. Here I broke down the steps to make it easy for you to note ingredients for all steps.

  • Chicken - 800 Gms
  • Oil - 1 ½ Tbsp

    • Whole Garam Masala:

  • Cumin Seeds: 1 Tsp, Cloves: 10, Green Cardamom: 6
  • Cinnamon: 3 inches, Black Pepper: 15
  • Ginger Garlic Paste - 3 Tsp
  • Salt - 1 Tsp
  • Red Chilli Powder - 1.5 Tsp
  • Turmeric Powder - 1 Tsp
  • Coriander Powder - 1 Tsp
  • Garam Masala Powder - 1.5 Tsp
  • Yogurt - 2 Tbsp
  • Brown Onion - 1 Tbsp
  • Tomatoes - 1 Cup (Without skin)
  • Water - 200 ml (for curry)

Rice Boil Ingredients

  • Rice 2 Cups

    • Garam Masala for Rice:

  • Bay Leaves: 2, Cloves: 5, Cinnamon: 2″
  • Cumin Seeds: 1 Tsp, Aniseed: 1
  • Salt - 2 Tsp (For Rice)

Step 1: Marination

  1. In a bowl, add ginger garlic paste, salt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder.
  2. Also, add garam masala powder and yogurt.
  3. Add crushed brown onion and mix well to make a blended paste of all spices.
  4. Place chicken in a bowl and give it a good mix.
  5. Marinate Chicken for 60 minutes.

Step 2: Making Curry

  1. Now in a pan, add oil and heat it up.
  2. Add whole garam masala. As it starts popping up, place marinated chicken and cook on high heat.
  3. As the chicken starts releasing water then add peeled tomatoes and cook on high heat.
  4. When oil starts to float on the surface then add 200 ml water and cover for 25 minutes to tender chicken on low/medium heat.

Step 3: Boiling Rice

  1. Soak basmati rice for ½ an hour.
  2. In a spacious pan, add 500 ml water.
  3. And add whole garam masala and salt.
  4. Cover for 10 minutes to make the water steamy and aromatic with gram masala flavor.
  5. Add rice in hot water and cook until it cooks 80%.
  6. Drain water from rice and start the layering process.

Step 4: Layering

  1. Generously brush oil in biryani pan on the bottom to avoid rice sticking.
  2. Now place a layer of rice and pour the chicken curry over it.
  3. At the same time, sprinkle fresh coriander and green chilies at the top of the curry layer.
  4. Now add 2nd layer of rice.
  5. Make another layer of chicken curry.
  6. Now again sprinkle green coriander.
  7. Cover the chicken top with remaining rice.
  8. Sprinkle red food color and mix well with the help of a spoon.
  9. Now squeeze fresh lemon.
  10. Sprinkle crushed brown onion.
  11. Pour a few drops of biryani essence.
  12. To make it more tempting and aromatic, pour saffron milk (In lukewarm milk (3 tsp), add saffron petals and leave it for 1 hour).
  13. Cover the pan and keep on high heat for 2 minutes.
  14. After 2 minutes, turn the heat to low to make it steamy. (Dum)
  15. Keep on dum for 20 minutes.
  16. After 20 minutes the Hyderabadi biryani will be ready to serve.
